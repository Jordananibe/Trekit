About Us - Trekit

Our Mission

At Trekit, our mission is to simplify campus navigation for everyone—whether you're a student, faculty member, or visitor. We believe that a well-organized campus should be easy to explore, and that's why we've developed a user-friendly platform designed to help you get around your campus quickly and efficiently.

Our Vision

Our goal is to provide a seamless campus experience. Trekit is here to ensure that no matter where you're headed—be it a lecture hall, a library, or the nearest restroom—you can always find the quickest, easiest route. Our interactive maps, real-time directions, and accessible paths are built to make your daily campus journey as simple as possible.

What We Do

Trekit offers a range of features that make campus navigation more intuitive:

  • Interactive Maps: View up-to-date and detailed maps of your campus, highlighting all major buildings, facilities, and routes.
  • Step-by-Step Directions: Get clear, concise, and real-time directions to any location on campus, whether it's a classroom, office, or restroom.
  • Building Information: Learn about campus facilities, including their amenities, opening hours, and contact details.
  • Accessibility Routes: We ensure everyone has access to campus by providing wheelchair-friendly paths, ramps, and entrances.
  • Live Updates: Stay informed about any changes in campus layouts or construction projects that might affect your usual routes

How Trekit Works

Using Trekit is easy! Here's how you can get started:

  1. Search for a Location: Type the building name, faculty, or place into the search bar.
  2. View Your Route: Instantly see a detailed path from your current location to your destination.
  3. Follow Step-by-Step Guidance: Receive real-time directions with walking paths and entry points to ensure you stay on track.
  4. Explore Additional Features: Tap on buildings to view facility details or find accessible routes when needed.

Why Choose Trekit?

We know that time on campus can be tight and getting lost can waste precious moments. Trekit is designed to guide you in real-time and make navigating your campus as effortless as possible. Whether you're on a tight schedule, new to the campus, or simply trying to find the quickest route, Trekit has you covered.
