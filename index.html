FAQs

Q: Do I need to download an app to use Trekit?
--- No. You can access Trekit directly through your web browswer on any device.

Q: Can I find accessible routes to locations?
--- Yes. Trekit highlights accessible paths.

Q: Can I use Trekit offline?
--- No. Trekit requires an internet connnection for live navigation and map updates.

Q: Is there a way to bookmark my frequently visited locations?
--- Yes, you can save your favourite places for quick access.

Q: Can Trekit help me if I'm visiting the campus for the first time?
--- Yes. Trekit is design for both new and returning users.

Q: Can I get directions for off-campus navigation?
--- No. Trekit gives information for buildings and location within the campus only.

Troubleshooting

  • Map not loading: Reloading the page or check internet connection.
  • Directions innacurate: Make sure your device's location services are enabled.
  • Can't find a building: Check the spelling or use the building's official name.
